# Assembly #

## Foot ##

1. Cut hole in squash ball
2. Mix FlexFloat-iT! 17
  a) put 5g part A in cup
  b) put 1.85x (9.2g) part B in cup
  c) mix for 10 seconds
3. Quickly pour between 6.2 and 6.6g into squash ball
4. Insert foot bracket
5. While sideways, spin gently for 4 seconds
6. Place plastic side up and weigh down with 500g weight
